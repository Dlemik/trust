Must-See Attractions in Taraz

Karakhan Mausoleum
Taraz, Kazakhstan
One of the most famous historical sites in Taraz, the Karakhan Mausoleum is a stunning example of medieval architecture.
Ayasha Bibi Mausoleum
Taraz, Kazakhstan
This mausoleum, built in honor of Ayasha Bibi, is known for its intricate tilework and serves as a symbol of eternal love.
Talas River
Taraz, Kazakhstan
A peaceful river that flows through Taraz, offering scenic views and relaxing picnic spots for visitors.
Ancient City Ruins
Taraz, Kazakhstan
Explore the ruins of the ancient city of Taraz, dating back over 2000 years, and uncover the region's rich history.
Shymbulak Park
Taraz, Kazakhstan
A beautiful park in Taraz where families can enjoy outdoor activities and scenic landscapes.
Akyrtas Palace
Taraz, Kazakhstan
Ancient ruins of the Akyrtas Palace, an architectural mystery with stone carvings and historical significance.
Zhaisan Monastery
Taraz, Kazakhstan
A serene monastery located outside Taraz, known for its peaceful ambiance and scenic surroundings.
Taraz Central Bazaar
Taraz, Kazakhstan
Experience the vibrant culture and local flavors of Taraz at the Central Bazaar, a bustling marketplace.
